<h2>Research Workflow for Humanities Scholars</h2>

<div class="card">
  <p style="font-size: 1.125rem; line-height: 1.7;">
    <strong>VIRENS</strong> (Verdant Inquiry Research Environment for Scholars) is an open-source, 
    modular framework that integrates macOS applications and automation tools into a unified 
    academic workflow—from source capture to publication.
  </p>
</div>

<div class="card">
  <h3>Core Philosophy</h3>
  <p>
    Built on the <a href="https://verdantinquiry.org">Verdant Inquiry</a> methodology, VIRENS 
    emphasizes <em>growth-oriented, inquiry-based scholarship</em> over productivity-focused 
    metrics. The system serves underresourced humanities scholars while maintaining strong 
    open-source principles and avoiding commercial productivity metaphors.
  </p>
</div>

<div class="card">
  <h3>The Trinity: Core Applications</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Obsidian</strong> — Knowledge synthesis and note-taking</li>
    <li><strong>Bookends</strong> — Citation management</li>
    <li><strong>DEVONthink</strong> — Document archive with AI-powered discovery</li>
  </ul>
  <p>
    Enhanced with automation through Keyboard Maestro, Hazel, Shortcuts, and scripting.
  </p>
</div>
