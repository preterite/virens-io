<section class="prospectus">
  <h1 id="page-title">Community</h1>
  <div class="prospectus-statement">
    <p>VIRENS is opinionated software. Scholars care about the ways we organize our work — our filing systems, our citation practices, our reading routines — because these methods shape what we can think. This forum offers a space to develop, test, and revise our methods, opinions, and commitments.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="prospectus-note">
    <p>VIRENS is in early alpha. The community is small and growing. That means your voice carries weight here — the project's direction is genuinely open to the people using it.</p>
  </div>
</section>
